About

Welcome to Ridhan Womens PG, where every woman is treated like family and provided with a safe and nurturing environment that feels just like home. Situated at C, 79A, Vinayagar Koil St, above A1 Parcel Service, Swarnapuri, Salem, Tamil Nadu 636004, our PG is designed to cater specifically to the needs and comforts of women.

Our facilities are thoughtfully designed to meet the needs of modern women, offering comfortable and well-furnished rooms, clean and hygienic living spaces, and round-the-clock security for peace of mind. Additionally, we provide nutritious and delicious home-cooked meals to ensure that our residents are well-nourished and energized.

But Ridhan Womens PG is more than just a place to stay – it's a community. We foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among our residents, encouraging friendship, support, and mutual respect. Whether you're a student, a working professional, or a homemaker, you'll find a welcoming and inclusive environment at Ridhan Womens PG.

Come and experience the warmth and hospitality of Ridhan Womens PG for yourself. Join our family and make memories that will last a lifetime. Your comfort and well-being are our top priorities, and we are committed to providing you with the best possible living experience.
